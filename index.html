Maintenance Management Website

Fig 1. Workflow of the website

Part of duty of my job is to maitaining and troubleshooting the production equipment. There are many parts are applied to the equipment; all of our parts were stored in the warehouse system with a number for us to order; sometimes, when we have issues on the equipment, finding the right parts to order is time comsuming. Therefore, I built a web page to reduce the time for us to looking for the parts number.
The management website allows our team member to store part to the system; when the part is created, the QR code with the parts infomation was created and ready to be printed; the printed QR code will be attatched in the related area for the equipment. It help us to make our job efficient.
In addition, the web page is able to access schematics and manuals to help us on troubleshooting; also provide better documental manage.
